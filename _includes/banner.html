Our Story

The fifth of June

My fiance proposed on June 5. He asked me to go to Zablai and at the same time my eldest sibling asked me as well to visit there after my work. Me and my family with my Fiance went there without knowing what will happen until He proposed to me in the scenery sunset in front of taal lake view deck. I was shocked, mixed emotions and happy that moment. Before that day I was sulking at him since I arrived at home first before him, without knowing before I arrived He was there already and asked permissions to my family for his intension to marry me.
