How to get into the area

Commute:

  • From Bus Terminal below SM Baguio, take Nolid North going to PTIX which take 5-6hrs commute.
  • From PTIX take a bus going to SM LIPA which will take 1-2hrs commute.
  • From SM Lipa take a jeep going to Taal Basilica, Church which take 1hr commute.
  • From Taal Basilica can ride a tricycle going to Via Elise Event Center its just 10mins away ride. You will see this arc and signage going to Via Elise Event Center

Private Vehicle: (See routes)

  • From Baguio take Marcos highway or Kennon road (weekend only) route going to Ayala ave which will take 5-6hrs ride depending on the peak hours time (Recommend to travel dawn time so there will be no traffic on the road).

    • You can pass TPLEX (optional if has traffic on the Mac Arthur highway) or pass Mac Arthur highway until SCETX
    • You can pass SCETX (optional if has traffic on Mac Arthur highway) or pass Mac Arthur highway until NLEX
    • If pass from Mac Arthur highway you need to take NLEX until EDSA or skyway until SLEX route

  • From Metro Manila EDSA or Skyway to Lipa, Batangas which wil take 1-2hrs travel

    • FROM EDSA travel until SLEX on ramp going to Lipa, Batangas
    • FROM NLEX - Skyway travel until SLEX on ramp going to Lipa, Batangas
  • FROM SLEX Lipa exit take Star Toll and exit Tambo exit.
  • From Tambo exit, take P Jose P. Laurel Highway going to Taal Basilica Church which will take 1hr travel
  • From Taal Basilica Church take Palico - Balayan road until Calle Ramon Diokno road until Taal - San Luis road (those roads are just straight connected road). See routes
  • From Taal - San Luis road ahead there will be a signage on the Left side of the road stating that Via Elise is ahead already and take right You will see this arc and signage going to Via Elise Event Center

See routes in google map

You can check or use waze as well just look for Taal Basilica and Via Elise Event Center
